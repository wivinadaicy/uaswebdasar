WHO IS RENCY?

Why We Here?

Rency is a woman’s fashion community which offers a wide range of fashion information from all over the world. The community was built in 2014 by two young ladies named Veren Valencia and Wivina Daicy. That’s why we give the name of this community as Rency. Because we take the name from the founders.
It is the world’s largest fashion community. Beside giving information and ideas about fashion. Rency also provide store to support your outfit collection. To boost up woman’s ideas and confident, Rency has a submission section that allow woman to share their outfit ideas by submit their photos. The purpose of it is giving opportunities to them to be the inspiration for others.

VISION

To become the number one fashion community that would inspire woman to learn about fashion sense based on worldwide trend

MISSION

1. To give an opportunity and encourages the creativity of each woman through style and fashion.
2.To provide fashion news and fashion store that support woman’s fashion ideas.
